\begin{abstract}
Traditional retail management systems focus on basic operations such as inventory and billing, with limited decision support. This SRS defines the requirements for an AI-powered retail shop management system that integrates a Large Language Model (LLM) with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to enable natural-language queries, automated insights, and improved decision making. The document specifies scope, functional and non-functional requirements, interfaces, constraints, and acceptance criteria in clear, formal language.
\end{abstract}
